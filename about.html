Grown from the Ground Up

A story of coffee, flour, and a very stubborn fig tree

Our Roots

The Old Framing Shop

Two years ago, this corner was filled with saw-dust and the smell of varnish. It was a local framing shop for decades, preserving memories in oak and glass. When we took over the space, we wanted to keep that spirit of craftsmanship alive—just with different tools.

The name "Fig & Filter" came naturally. Tucked away in our back courtyard stands a massive, gnarled fig tree that has outlasted every business on this block. It's our anchor, providing shade for our guests and a constant reminder that the best things take time to grow.

Meet the Makers

Mara

Founding Baker

Mara is the silent engine of the shop. Every morning at 5:40 AM, she begins the ritual of folding dough and firing the ovens. For her, baking is a conversation with the ingredients.

Dev

Master Roaster

Dev sources his beans directly from small estates. He spends his Tuesdays and Fridays in the roasting room, chasing the perfect profile for our seasonal blends.
