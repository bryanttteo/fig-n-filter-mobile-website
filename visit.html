Public Transit

We are a short 4-minute walk from the Weaver Street Metro Station (Blue Line). Several bus lines also stop at the corner of Chalk Lane.

Parking

Limited 1-hour street parking is available on Chalk Lane. For longer visits, we recommend the multi-story car park on Union Street, just two blocks away.
